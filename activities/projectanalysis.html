Truffles and Trifles. Website Re-Design

Designer: Linda Kounechongprasert

Site Intent

For this re-design project, the intent will be to create a more appealing and uniform UI. The focus will be placed on reorganizing page layout as well as choosing fonts and colors for the site that will compliment each other and make for a better user experience. The goal is to get more users to visit the site and browse. This will give them a chance to see what the company has to offer and lead them to possibly book a cooking class or purchase a gift basket.

Another focus of the re-design will be to condense the site and remove some of the unneccessary pages. The completed site will consist of 6 well organized and informative pages. The layout will be designed to make browsing the site easier and inviting. The main navigation will be placed in a more accessible place on the page and a call to action will be created for the home page, inviting users to book a cooking class.

Competitive Analysis

Truffles and Trifles offers cooking classes as well as gift baskets and baked goods. This gives them the competitive edge because they offer more than one product/service from their business but it also means they have more types of competitors. The top local competitors within each category include:

www.gourmetmagic.org

Gourmetmagic.org is based out of Windermere, Fl and offers cooking and team building classes. They have a global ranking of 26,123,990 on Alexa.com and there are 2 sites that link into it. 61.30% of those searching for "orlando cooking classes" are driving to this site compared to the 1.41% that is driven to Truffles and Trifles.

www.basketsorlando.com

Basketsorlando.com is base out of Orlando, Fl and they offer custom gift baskets. They deliver within the Orlando area and also offer nationwide shipping. There is not enough data on Alexa.com to give them a ranking but it does show that they have 4 sites linking in. They also received a #2 ranking on a list of best gift baskets in the Orlando area.

www.charliesgourmetpastries.com

Charliesgourmetpastries.com is based out of Orlando, Fl and they offer gourmet pastries and baked goods. They have a global ranking of 8,528,121 on Alexa.com and there are 16 sites linking into it. 23.70% of the traffic searching "gourmet bakery orlando fl" is driven to this site compared to 0% that is driven to Truffles and Trifles.
